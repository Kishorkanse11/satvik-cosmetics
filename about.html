What We Don't Use

Parabens

Harmful preservatives that can disrupt hormones and cause skin irritation.

Sulfates

Harsh detergents that strip natural oils and damage skin barrier.

Silicones

Synthetic fillers that can clog pores and prevent skin from breathing.

Lead & Heavy Metals

Toxic substances that accumulate in the body and cause long-term damage.

Carmine

Animal-derived colorant we avoid for ethical and vegan principles.

Mineral Oils

Petrolatum-based oils that can be contaminated and clog pores.
